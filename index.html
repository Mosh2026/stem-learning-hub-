Grade 6: Fractions & Decimals

Master parts of a whole and arithmetic operations.

  • Multiplying/Dividing Fractions
  • Ratios and Unit Rates

Practice:

  1. 1/2 + 1/4 = ?
  2. Convert 0.75 to fraction.
  3. 60 miles in 2 hours = ? mph

Grade 7: Ratios & Proportions

Explore unit rates, percentages, and basic geometry.

Practice:

  1. Solve: 3/5 = x/20
  2. 15% of $80 = ?
  3. Circumference (r=7cm) = ?

Grade 8: Linear Equations

Understand functions, slope, and the Pythagorean theorem.

Practice:

  1. Solve: 2x + 5 = 15
  2. Hypotenuse (legs 3cm, 4cm) = ?
  3. Slope of y = 4x - 2?

Grade 9: Algebra I

Polynomials, factoring, and quadratic functions.

Practice:

  1. Factor: x² + 5x + 6
  2. Solve: x+y=10, x-y=2
  3. Vertex of y=(x-3)²+4?
